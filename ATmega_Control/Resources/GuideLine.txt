Firstly, you must have to upload a dedicated HEX file to communicate with your ATmega board using this application.

Click the "Upload (.hex)" button.

If your ATmega Board is already Connected, you will be asked to disconnect.

If your ATmega Board is already Disconnected, stay disconnected.

Select your Microcontroller Chip.

Browse the necessary files properly.

Then click the "Find Port" button. A message will appear and ask you to connect your ATmega Board within 30 seconds.

After connecting properly, you will see the connected port. 

Click the "Flash" button.

It will flash the dedicated HEX file to your ATmega Board.

You must select the output pin from the pin list.

Now you can operate the functions (On, Off, Blink).

You can also upload any HEX file for ATmega Board.
